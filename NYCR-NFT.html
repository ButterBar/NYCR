NFT

We are 5000 rats from across NYC, together making something with the ERC721 blockchain as NFTs from our city expeiences. Each NFT is unique, using over 60 unique attributes. 

We love the eccentric art, chaotic street, and places we rats are not allowed to go. Everyday we wander around subway railway looking for pizza. At night we dwell in soft trash bags, count stars and talk about dreams and this never-ending virus. Generally it’s very rat- friendly. So we’d like to welcome our fellow rodents with special new york experience. Don’t worry, some good ones. We know the city like the back of our paws.

Roadmap

1
Each NFT is an entry to raffles, where you can win special NYC experience during NFT NYC.

800 mint out -> 1st raffle -> 5 winners
2000 mint out -> 2nd raffle -> 5 winners
5000 mint out -> 3rd raffle -> 5 winners
around NFT NYC -> 4th raffle -> 15 winners

Every raffle generates unique winners (wallet addresses) while excludes previous winners. 
We will then collect all 30 winners’ choices between following prizes. Hotel and restaurant include a plus-one. 

1. 3 nights in a 5-star hotel 
2. 1 meal in a Michelin-star restaurant 
3. 1 gift card for Saks Fifth Ave 

Only 10 places are available for each prize. Details will be released when time comes.

2
When it is getting close to NFT NYC, we will give away another 30 “all you can drink” for a roof-top bar.

3
Don’t get upset if you are NOT coming to NYC. After the event, we will continue giving away gifts to our holders. Eventually 90% money in the treasury wallet will be gifted away.
